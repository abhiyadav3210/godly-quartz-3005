What is a perk?

Many campaigns offer perks to say thanks to backers, in exchange for different contribution amounts. Perks can be objects, acknowledgement, a thank you, services, events or anything that does not violate our Terms of Use.

Perks are listed on the right side of the Campaign page, under the "Back It" button. If you do not see any perks listed, the campaign owner may have decided not to offer any perks.

Just a few of our favorite campaigns

Misfit Shine

7,957 Backers

With super-smart fitness technology squeezed into a sleek, take-it-anywhere design, the Misfit Shine was a huge hit. Today it’s available in stores everywhere, but Indiegogo backers were in on the action first.

Bluesmart

10,984 Backers

This smash-hit campaign almost singlehandedly invented the smart suitcase as the must-have travel accessory that it is today. With innovative features like location tracking and a built-in scale, Bluesmart went big on Indiegogo before landing in stores around the world. InDemand Campaign

Evapolar

6,616 Backers

The Evapolar team’s slick air conditioner is small enough to place exactly where you need it most. It even cleans and humidifies as it cools. The campaign was so successful that the team came back to Indiegogo to launch the next-gen version. InDemand Campaign

Super Troopers 2

54,531 Backers

With its truly gigantic backer community, Super Troopers 2 became one of the most iconic Indiegogo film projects. The team behind the project offered backers everything from movie tickets to an actual police car from the film! InDemand Campaign
